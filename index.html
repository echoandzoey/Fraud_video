What is AI Fraud?

Artificial Intelligence has revolutionized many aspects of our lives, but it has also given rise to new forms of fraud. Scammers are increasingly using AI technology to create sophisticated scams that can be difficult to detect.

Common AI Fraud Types:

  • Deepfake Videos and Audio
  • AI-Generated Phishing Emails
  • Automated Social Engineering
  • Voice Cloning Scams
